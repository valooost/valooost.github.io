How we use VR in lesson

Tested in the Ohm High School, Erlangen. We, the new Media work group, are happy to present our newest milestone in using VR in lessons: The first usage. With the allowity of a geography prof, Mr. Spies, we used Vive VR Glasses in his lesson. In thanks to Steam VR/Google Earth VR, we were able to show students the earth in VR mode.
